About FROST

FROST is a curated watch reselling brand built on precision, authenticity, and trust. We specialize in sourcing, verifying, and delivering high-quality timepieces — from timeless classics to modern icons — to collectors and enthusiasts around the world. At FROST, every watch is more than just a product; it’s a statement of craftsmanship, legacy, and personal style.

Brand Identity

FROST represents sharpness, clarity, and distinction. Our brand name reflects our clean aesthetic and meticulous attention to detail — just like the pieces we curate. We value transparency, timelessness, and a deep respect for the heritage of horology. Whether you're looking to invest, upgrade, or start your collection, FROST is your reliable source for authentic, verified watches.
